    Vivo Y21T Smartphone (4/128GB)

    The Vivo Y21T has a 6.51-inch HD+ IPS LCD screen. It features a Full-View waterdrop notch. The front-facing camera has an 8-megapixel resolution. The Vivo Y21T features a 5000 mAh battery with 18W rapid charging. It has 4 GB of Memory, an octa-core CPU that runs at up to 2.4 GHz, and an Adreno 610 GPU. The Qualcomm Snapdragon 680 4G (6 nm) chipset powers it. The smartphone has 64 GB of internal storage as well as a dedicated MicroSD slot. This phone has a fingerprint sensor on the side.

    The Vivo Y21T comes with a 2.5D slim body and a flat frame. Thanks to the curvature and a right-angled frame, it's sleek to look at and comfortable to hold. It has a 6.51-inch Halo FullView Display with HD+ (1600 × 720) resolution, providing you with a wide immersive view and sharp images. The display automatically adjusts the brightness based on ambient light conditions and filters out harmful blue light to prevent eye strain.

    Vivo Y21T integrates the fingerprint scanner with the power button so you can turn on the display and unlock your phone at the same time. This design makes Y21T look sleek and classy, and makes user interaction nearly effortless. The time it takes to unlock from a lit screen is a mere 0.232 seconds.

    The Vivo Y21T is powered by a Snapdragon 680 processor and boasts a large core with a CPU speed as high as 2.4GHz. It has an AnTuTu score of +20% compared with the previous generation. It comes with Adreno GPU 610 High-Quality Graphics.
